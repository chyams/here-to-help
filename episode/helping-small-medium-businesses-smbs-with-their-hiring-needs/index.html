Helping small & medium businesses (SMBs) with their hiring needs

Anna Rowe May 24, 2021
In this episode of Here to Help, host and CEO of Indeed, Chris Hyams, speaks to Anna Rowe, Indeed’s Senior Director of User Experience.Anna shares how her background in psychology helps her to really understand the people behind the businesses, and how Indeed can create tools and products to help SMB’s with their hiring needs.
