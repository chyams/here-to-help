How can we help veterans get into the workforce?

Solomon Rhima November 16, 2021
November is National Veterans and Military Families Month, in addition to Indeed’s Veterans & Allies Inclusion Resource Group (IRG) identity month.This week on Here to Help, Maggie Hulce, Indeed’s Senior Vice President (SVP) and General Manager (GM) of Enterprise, takes Indeed CEO Chris Hyams’ seat and hosts the show. She speaks to Solomon Rhima, a Marine Corps veteran and a senior sales specialist at Indeed with a focus on inclusive hiring.Maggie — the spouse of a veteran — catches up with Solomon, who after serving in the military for over 12 years, has spent his civilian career helping companies to hire veterans. Indeed will now recognize November 11 as an Indeed holiday for both Veterans Day (U.S.) and Remembrance Day (EMEA).
