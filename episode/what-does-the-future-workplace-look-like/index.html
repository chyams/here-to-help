What does the future workplace look like?

Yaeji Myung May 3, 2022
This week on Here to Help, Chris is joined by Yaeji Myung, Workplace Strategies Manager at Indeed to discuss the future of the Indeed workplace. As "Indeedians" begin to return to offices all over the world, Chris and Yaeji discuss the new ways that the workplace strategy team is thinking about flexibility and the hybrid work environment.Yaeji joined Indeed just before the pandemic sent all Indeedians to work from home, and she reflects on how the changes and pivots in this great work-from-home experiment has influenced her approach to workplace strategy.
