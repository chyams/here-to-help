Understanding the person behind the job search

Muna Hussaini January 20, 2021
To kick-off a fresh new season of Here to Help, Indeed’s CEO, Chris Hyams is joined by Muna Hussaini, Chief of Staff to Indeed’s Jobseeker Organization.Muna, who has worked in tech for almost 20 years, speaks about her personal connection to Indeed’s mission of Helping ALL People Get Jobs, and why equity is so important when understanding the mindset of job seekers.
