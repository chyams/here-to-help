Honoring family caregivers across the world

Chris Powell November 19, 2021
In honor of National Family Caregivers Month, this episode features Indeed CEO Chris Hyams and Chris Powell, Director of IT at Indeed.Chris Powell is a father to his daughter and a caretaker to his wife, Blythe, who was diagnosed with brain cancer earlier this year. As a parent and caregiver, Chris speaks to how he must balance authenticity and vulnerability – while still managing to help people get jobs.Trigger Warning: Some of the topics covered in this episode may be upsetting to listeners, such as bereavement and suicide.
