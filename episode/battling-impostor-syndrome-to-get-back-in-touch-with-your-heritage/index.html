Battling impostor syndrome to get back in touch with your heritage

Adrienne Smith September 20, 2021
National Hispanic Heritage Month runs from September 15 to October 15, and during this period, we recognize the presence and influence of the Latinx culture in our society.In this episode of Here to Help, Indeed CEO Chris Hyams is joined by Adrienne Smith, Global Training Strategist and co-chair of Latinx in Tech at Indeed. Adrienne will discuss her experiences growing up in Georgia and battling imposter syndrome to get back in touch with her heritage. She also speaks to the importance of recognizing the multiplicity of Us/Them dichotomies.
