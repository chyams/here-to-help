Understanding the work of Indeed University

Heather Wood December 9, 2020
Indeed University (IU) is an immersive onboarding program for new college grads from 40+ universities across the world. The goal of IU is to teach our new hires about data-driven decision-making by having them build new startups within Indeed.In this episode of Here to Help, Indeed’s CEO Chris Hyams interviewed Heather Wood, a Senior Software Engineering Manager. The pair discuss her experience as a Dean of Indeed University this year -- and how IU reflected our core value of ‘Innovation’ in light of the unique challenges that 2020 presented.
