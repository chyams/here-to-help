Why is asking the important questions so hard?

Gyasi Barber February 17, 2022
Black History Month kicked off on Feb 1, 2022 and runs until Mar 1, 2022. It is a time to observe the contributions and accomplishments of Black people throughout history.Gyasi Barber is the first guest in February to join Chris to observe Black History Month at Indeed. Gyasi pursued a career in opera and trained for years in the vocal arts before pivoting to a career in sales, eventually leading him to a role at Indeed in New York.Chris and Gyasi delve into the importance of a “brave space” in times of change and disruption, and why it’s important to have the courage to ask questions. They also chat about Black History Month and the Black Lives Matter movement, and why “belonging” matters more than you think…
