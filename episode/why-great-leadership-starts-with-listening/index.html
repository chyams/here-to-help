Why great leadership starts with listening

Laurie Williams October 13, 2021
Laurie Williams is a Senior Product Manager at Indeed, the Dean of Indeed University, a veteran, and a mother of four kids and three yappy dogs.In this episode of Here to Help, Chris sits down with Laurie to learn about her time serving in Korea and Kuwait to working in Zappos and Riot Games. Laurie's career shapes her view of leadership and she will talk about why she is passionate about creating strong and effective teams — and helping people do their best work together.
