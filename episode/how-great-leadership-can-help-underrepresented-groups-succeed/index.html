How great leadership can help underrepresented groups succeed

Mary Bui-Pham March 30, 2021
This week, Chris is joined by Mary Bui-Pham, Indeed's VP of Software Engineering.Mary will speak about her journey from academia and chemical engineering to Indeed, how she supports women in tech; her love for orchids, and what they teach you about leadership.
