Best of: How can behavioral science create more equity in hiring?

Rachel Rosenberg January 3, 2023
In this episode of Here to Help, Indeed CEO Chris Hyams speaks to Rachel Rosenberg, a behavioral scientist who is passionate about designing products and services for social impact. Rachel joined Indeed as the first product-focused behavioral scientist at Indeed, and her focus is on helping people find meaningful work — and employers find the best diverse talent to grow their businesses.Chris and Rachel cover everything from behavioral economics and job search to challenging unconscious bias in recruitment.
