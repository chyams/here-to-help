Building a more equitable recovery

Steve Preston September 17, 2020
This episode of Here to Help will feature Chris Hyams and Steve Preston, CEO of Goodwill Industries International (GII).Steve and Chris will talk about our shared mission for helping all job seekers get jobs, the impact COVID-19 has had on Goodwill's operations and how the pandemic has disproportionately impacted job seekers with barriers. Finally, they will also explore how we might use the lessons learned from the current economic crisis, to build a more inclusive recovery for all.
