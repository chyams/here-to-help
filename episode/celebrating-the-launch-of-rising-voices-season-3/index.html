Celebrating the launch of Rising Voices Season 3

Lena Waithe & Rishi Rajani June 13, 2022
Recorded live at the Tribeca Film Festival, Chris Hyams sits down with Emmy Award-winning writer, producer, and actor Lena Waithe and Hillman Grad Productions CEO Rishi Rajani to celebrate the launch of Rising Voices Season 3. They discuss how Indeed's Rising Voices program supports the next generation of BIPOC filmmakers by providing mentorship, full production support, and $100,000 in funding to each selected director. Lena and Rishi share their passion for protest art, the importance of mentorship over mere shadowing, and how Rising Voices demonstrates Indeed's belief that talent is universal, but opportunity is not.
